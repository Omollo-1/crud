About Chartitze

Our story, mission, and the team behind our work

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Sarah Johnson, Chartitze began as a small shelter for 5 orphaned children in Nairobi, Kenya. Sarah, a former social worker, saw the desperate need for a safe, loving environment for children who had lost their parents.

What started as a modest initiative has grown into a comprehensive support system for orphaned children across three regions. Today, we provide holistic care to hundreds of children every year, helping them heal, learn, and dream of a brighter future.

Our Mission

To provide orphaned children with a loving home, quality education, healthcare, and emotional support, empowering them to become confident, self-sufficient adults who contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

A world where every orphaned child has access to love, care, and opportunities to realize their full potential, regardless of their circumstances.

Our Values

Compassion

We approach every child with empathy, kindness, and understanding.

Safety

We create secure environments where children feel protected and valued.

Education

We believe education is the foundation for a better future.

Community

We foster a sense of belonging and connection for every child.
