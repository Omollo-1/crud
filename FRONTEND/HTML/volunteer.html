Why Volunteer With Us?

Our volunteers are the heart of Chartitze. By sharing your time and skills, you directly impact the lives of orphaned children and help create lasting change.

Benefits of Volunteering

  • Make a tangible difference in children's lives
  • Gain valuable experience in child welfare
  • Join a supportive community
  • Flexible scheduling options
  • Training and ongoing support

Volunteer Roles Available

Tutoring & Teaching

Help children with homework and educational activities

Meal Preparation

Assist with cooking and serving nutritious meals

Recreational Activities

Organize games, sports, and creative activities

Mentoring

Provide guidance and emotional support to children
