Our Programs

Comprehensive support for every child's needs

Education

We provide quality education through our on-site school, tutoring programs, and scholarships for higher education. Our curriculum focuses on academic excellence and life skills.

  • Primary and secondary education
  • Computer literacy classes
  • Vocational training programs
  • University scholarships

Healthcare

Regular health checkups, nutritional support, mental health counseling, and access to medical care ensure our children grow up healthy and strong.

  • Regular medical checkups
  • Nutritional meals program
  • Mental health counseling
  • Dental and vision care

Mentorship

Our mentorship program connects children with caring adults who provide guidance, emotional support, and positive role modeling for personal development.

  • One-on-one mentorship
  • Career guidance
  • Life skills workshops
  • Emotional support groups

Nutrition

We provide balanced, nutritious meals daily and educate children about healthy eating habits to support their physical and cognitive development.

  • Three balanced meals daily
  • Nutrition education
  • Our own vegetable garden
  • Supplemental feeding program
  • Clean water access
